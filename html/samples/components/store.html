Store Preferences

Store Preference
Store user or global preferences in your preferred TM1 cube. Ensure that it is a writable String cell. This directive can store any type of JSON data such as for example {"name":"John", "age":25}. To store a short string such as month or year it is recommended to use the directive tm1-ui-dbr instead. Depending on your connection to your TM1 server, type with a speed of at most, 1-character-per-second! Or better yet, just paste it there.

If needed, you can adjust the time it will take before the store will push the changes into TM1 via tm1-save-delay.
